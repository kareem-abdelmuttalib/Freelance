Frequently asked questions

Quick answers
How do I request a venue for my club?

Submit a booking via the Booking page with your preferred slot; SAR suggests alternates automatically.

Can I edit a booking after submission?

Yes, resubmit the form with the updated details; coordinators receive a diff and re-approve quickly.

Where do I see my past events?

Head to History to view cards and open modal details with photos and notes.

Who can approve activities?

Faculty advisors and activity coordinators receive notifications and can respond within 24 hours.
